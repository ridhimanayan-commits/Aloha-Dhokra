The Craft Detail

The Architecture of Wax

Dhokra is a non-ferrous metal casting using the lost-wax technique. This sort of metal casting has been used in India for over 4,000 years and is still used. One of the earliest known lost wax artifacts is the dancing girl of Mohenjo-daro.
