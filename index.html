The Lost Wax Process

01

Clay Core

A core is formed from river clay and dried in the sun.

02

Wax Threading

Beeswax and resin are drawn into wires and wrapped around the core.

03

Clay Mold

A thick layer of clay seals the wax structure completely.

04

Firing

The mold is heated. The wax melts and flows out, leaving a cavity.

05

Casting

Molten scrap brass is poured into the cavity, cooled, and the mold is broken.
