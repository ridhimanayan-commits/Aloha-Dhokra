The Lost Wax Process

An ancient metallurgical technique where the mold is destroyed to birth the art.

01

Clay Core

A core is formed from river clay and dried in the sun, creating the base structure.

02

Wax Threading

Beeswax and resin are drawn into wires and wrapped around the core with intense precision.

03

Clay Mold

A thick layer of fine clay and earth seals the delicate wax structure completely.

04

Firing

The mold is heated in an open furnace. The wax melts and flows out, leaving an empty cavity.

05

Casting

Molten scrap brass is poured into the cavity, cooled, and finally, the mold is broken to reveal the art.
